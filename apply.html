Student Application

The requirements for offline and online application are;

Secondary Section

Form 1

  • A photocopy of the Acceptance slip
  • Class 6 report card or primary school report card
  • Photocopy of birth certificate
  • An interview fee of 1000 frs (to be payed on the interview day)

Form 2 - Form 4

  • Previous Class report card
  • Photocopy of birth certificate
  • An interview fee of 1000 Frs (to be payed on the interview day)

Lowersixth

  • GCE O/L result slip or printed page of the result
  • Form 5 report card
  • Photocopy of birth certificate
  • An interview fee of 1000 Frs (to be payed on the interview day)

Primary Section

Class 1

  • A photocopy of the child's clinial card showing the vaccination taken
  • Nursery school certificate
  • A photocopy of birth certificate
  • A registration fee of 5000 Frs (to be payed in school before admission into class)

Class 2 - Class 6

  • Previous Class report card
  • Photocopy of birth certificate
  • A registration fee of 5000 Frs (to be payed in school before admission into class)

Nursery Section

Pre-Nursery and Nursery 1

  • A photocopy of the child's clinial card showing the vaccination taken
  • A photocopy of birth certificate
  • A registration fee of 5000 Frs (to be payed in school before admission into class)

Note: Entry age is 2yrs for Pre-Nursery and 3yrs for Nursery 1

Note: Students are not admitted in Form 5 and Uppersixth

Special Requirements for the primary and secondary sections

  • 2 Pairs of Daily Uniforms
  • 2 Pairs of pullovers
  • 1 Official uniform
  • 1 Rainbow sport wear
  • Transport Fare: 43,000Frs

Teacher Application

The requirements for offline and online application are;

  • A hand written application
  • A photocopy of the highest certificate
  • A CV
  • A photocopy of the national ID
  • A 4x4 picture on a white or red background

RAINBOW COMPLEX

Rainbow Bilingual Complex is the most prestigious primary school in the city of Dschang. Our dream was and remains to have a school where both the children of the rich and the poor could study together in a good atmosphere. Building on a proud tradition, Rainbow Bilingual complex fosters excellence in its students, providing a meaningful and fulfilling education across three campuses.
