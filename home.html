COMMUNITY

The Rainbow Bilingual School Complex Community goes beyond education. In the evening our students do sports, make music and explore their environment together, thereby strengthening their team skills.

Reading Club

Having fun while reading – in a playful manner and based on the scientifically acknowledged Immersion Method.

Science Club

Transform Thoeries to Practices through several easy experiments

Jambory Club

Building up a better sense of body and rhythm and training their flexibility - Jambory is about much more than just dancing..

Music Club

Dive into the world of music and get to know various instruments such as the recorder, keyboard, percussion instruments and also your own voice.

Building on a proud tradition, Rainbow Bilingual complex fosters excellence in its students, providing a meaningful and fulfilling education across three campuses.
